What Is This?

The Multiplication Table Generator is a free online math tool that lets you instantly create a times table for any number from 1 to 100,000. Unlike static printed charts, you control the range — set it to start from any number and go up to any number you need.

Once your table is generated, you can print it directly from your browser or save it as a PDF with a single click — clean, formatted, and ready for classroom or homework use.

Who Is It For?

  • Students (Grade 2–8): Practice and print your times tables for homework, revision, or exam prep.
  • Teachers: Generate custom printable worksheets for any topic — from basic 2× to advanced 999×.
  • Parents: Help your child learn at home with clear, distraction-free printable tables.
  • Tutors: Quickly produce tailored practice materials for different student levels.
  • Anyone curious: Need to know what 347 × 92 is in a table format? This tool does that too.
